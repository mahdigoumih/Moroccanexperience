Fixed Departures

Our small group trips offer guests an immersive exploration of Morocco at a budget-friendly price. These trips follow a pre-arranged 8-night itinerary with no more than 10 travelers.

Trips depart on the first Saturday of every month — even with just one participant, so there is no need to worry about reaching a minimum.

  • Max 10 travelers per group
  • 8-night curated itinerary
  • Departures every first Saturday
  • Pre & post-trip extensions available

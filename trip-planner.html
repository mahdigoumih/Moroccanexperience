Morocco travel tips
01
The best months to visit are October–April when temperatures are perfect for sightseeing and desert nights.
02
Always negotiate prices in souks — it's expected and part of the culture. Start at 40% of the asking price.
03
Dress modestly outside of resorts. Shoulders and knees covered is respectful and appreciated.
04
Learn a few Darija phrases: "Shukran" (thank you), "Bsahtek" (cheers), "La, shukran" (no, thank you).
05
Friday is the holy day — medinas and souks are quieter in the morning but lively after midday prayers.
